Read This (Important)


- Landlords are the parties responsible for drafting the Contract, they enter the details of contract as discussed with the Tenants and follow guidelines defined by the government.

- The contracts have details like Ethereum Address of the Tenant, Address of the Property, Duration, Rent, Security Deposits and Other Terms of Agreement.

- Landlords have to pay the Registration Fee defined by Government at the time of drafting the contract, it will be refuded back if the Contract is rejected by either the Tenant or the Government.

- After the deployment of contract, the tenants will verify and approve the Contract, they are also required to pay the Security Deposit at the same step which will be held in an Escrow Account and released only when both parties give their approval.

- After the Tenant's Approval, third step would be Government's Verification of the Contract, with the completion of this step the contract will become Active and deployed on the Blockchain.
